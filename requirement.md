# ABOUT US PAGE

## Welcome to AI Professional College

AI Professional College was established in 2014.

The institution offers career development programs designed around industry demands and emerging economic trends.

The goal is to help students:

* Develop career-focused skills
* Improve their quality of life
* Impact society positively
* Become business and technology experts

Teaching staff are drawn from experienced professionals working in leading organizations.

The institution states that it:

* Provides equal educational opportunities
* Does not discriminate
* Focuses on practical education
* Provides pathways for career advancement

### Admission Requirement Mentioned

Entry level qualification:

* Five credits
* Maximum two sittings
* Minimum C5 in English Language
* WASSCE or GCE O-Level
