# AI Professional College

Welcome to the **AI Professional College** project repository. This platform is designed to provide industry-focused career development, degree programs, and practical professional training.

## About the Project
AI Professional College offers a wide range of academic and professional courses aimed at equipping students with practical, industry-relevant skills. The website provides detailed course information, an integrated application system, and details about the college's affiliations and leadership.

## Programs Offered

### Degree Courses
- Computer Science
- Information Technology
- Business Administration
- Accounting & Economics
- Human Resource Management
- Public Health & Nursing (S.R.N)
- Logistics & Supply Chain Management

### Professional Courses
- **Finance & Accounting:** ACCA, CIMA
- **Business Management:** ABE
- **IT Infrastructure:** COMPTIA A+, N+, S+, CISCO CCNA, CCNP
- **Software Training:** Database Management, Cloud Infrastructure, Microsoft Certifications

## Features
- **Modern Responsive UI:** Built with HTML, CSS, and Bootstrap.
- **Application Portal:** Dedicated multi-step wizard for Degree applications (`register.html`) and a simplified professional application form (`register-professional.html`).
- **Partner Affiliations:** Proudly affiliated with Njala University, Ernest Bai Koroma University, and St. Monica College.
