News Detail

  • News
  • / Detail

AI Professional College is proud to announce the opening of our state-of-the-art AI Laboratory. Designed to give students hands-on experience with cutting-edge technologies, the lab is equipped with high-performance computing resources and industry-standard software.

This initiative is part of our commitment to producing career-ready graduates who can tackle complex problems in the modern digital economy. The lab will support programs in Computer Science, Data Analytics, and Software Engineering.

A Hub for Innovation

Students and faculty will collaborate on research projects, hackathons, and industry partnerships within this new facility. We believe this addition will significantly enhance the practical skills of our students.
